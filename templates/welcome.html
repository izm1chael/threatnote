• Product Intro
    Walkthrough

    This quick tutorial will mention a few of the key items you should get familiar with to use threatnote.io effectively. Sit back and enjoy the quick show!

  • Intelligence
    Requirements

    Intel Requirements are the first stop you should take. Use our handy-dandy IR wizard to help craft requirements for your intelligence collection, analysis and production. Identify stakeholders, gaps, deliverables and more!

  • Reports

    Reports are generated in Markdown and seek to answer the gaps identified in your intelligence requirements. Use this ad-hoc as well to write-up any related threat activity as well, and have our indicator parsers do their magic and automatically enrich these bad boys.

  • Dashboards
    and Consumers

    Get some sweet dashboards to help track progress, as well as a page to manage your Consumers of intelligence. This will help you keep track of who gets what and what your interactions with each team look like.

  • Communication
    Tools

    Feel free to leave a comment under any Indicator, Report, or Intelligence Requirement for others to comment back on. This is a quick and easy way to stay connected and on track!
